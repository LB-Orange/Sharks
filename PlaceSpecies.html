Coral Reefs

Coral reefs are excellent locations for sharks as they are very biodiverse. In coral reefs, various species can either coexist with sharks or act as food sources.

Some sharks that live by coral reefs include:
1)Blacktip Reef Sharks
2)Nurse Sharks
3)Blue Sharks

Arctic

Arctic environments contain many large mammals such as seals, an excellent food source for larger sharks.

Some sharks that live by the arctic include:
1)Greenland Shark
2)Great White Sharks
3)Salmon Shark

New York City

NYC has about 26 species of sharks, as NYC is near the Hudson Canyon.

Some sharks that live by New York City include:
1)Sand Tiger Sharks
2)Sandbar Sharks
3)Dogfish Sharks
